# Shibiu
Shibu was an interesting Minecraft ctf game. I was a bit unskilled at first, but I came back with new energy

I received a zip containing the Shibuya map, I checked the configuration using nbt explorer and compared it to the original map, "Shibuya Recreation. Sort of..." by Noshychan, but I didn't find anything interesting. Everything seems fine.. I tried going to specific coordinates listed in the *playerdata* file, but found nothing. So, I decided to install Wrust and explore the area. I stumbled upon some flags and, thinking they were part of the challenge, removed the entire layer of grass around them... but after 30 minutes of searching using X-ray, the search function for signs, command blocks , and ESP for chests, I realized I was looking at the wrong map. I had been searching on the original map, not the one intended for the challenge. I switched to the correct map, checked again with X-ray, and found the flag.
